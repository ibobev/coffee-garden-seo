Coffee Shops Information

Above are our current coffee shop locations. We are constantly improving and trying to expand to other locations. In case you find it difficult to visit us and try our coffee - we make home deliveries. Use the provided phone numbers at any of our shops to make a delivery request. Keep in mind that a delivery can take up to an hour or so depending on your location and city traffic.

At this moment we only provide this service in the city of Sofia. However, regarding our merch(coffee mugs, roasted bean packages) we can deliver outside of the city within the country borders. We are also working on our mobile delivery app, you can expect it later this year.

In regard of the pandemic, all the necessary precautions are taken in order for our customers to enjoy their coffee without disturbance. We work at 50% capacity on the spot and disinfect hourly. If you - the customer, have any suggestions regarding our services feel free to contact us at any time. Stay safe.
