Coffee Garden's history

About Us

Coffee Garden was established in 2014 by a group of three coffee enthusiasts. Things back then were different, the company was yet to be recognized as a brand of high quality coffee at an affordable price for everyone to enjoy. The moment that changed the course of hisotry for Coffee Garden was back in 2016, when the founders started importing coffee beans from all around the world, experimenting with different coffee sorts and improving the quality of their products - still accessible to their regulars.

In 2018 the menu was full of exotic coffee which undoubtedly put spotlight on the company, later that year Coffee Garden became one of the most recognizible coffee shop company worldwide.

If you want to learn more about Coffee Garden's history or have any questions - you can contact us.
