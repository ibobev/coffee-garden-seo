Our Dedication Towards Coffee Brewing

Learn about Coffee Garden's coffee

We, at Coffee Garden, strive to deliver our customers the highest coffee quality! Our dedication towards coffee brewing is tremendous. Coffee brewing for us is in its own way an art. Every cup served has a story, the coffee beans used to make coffee have been thoroughly inspected and only the finest reach our baristas to make sure they fit our standard, thus making sure the coffee you tried your first time at our facilities is as always extraordinary.


Coffee Garden offers a vast majority of beans from all the known and not so known coffee plant sorts(including Arabica, Robusta, Liberica and others if you are daring). We import from three continets - Africa, South America and Asia, mainly from Brazil, Indonesia, Ethiopia and others. The beans that reach your coffee cup are only the highest quality ones.


For us coffee is more than just a hot caffeine drink. It is life, it is love, it is art and every coffee person deserves to try a premium one. And why not make it accessible to everyone all the time? If you are wondering just that - wonder no more because this is Coffee Gardens purpose.
